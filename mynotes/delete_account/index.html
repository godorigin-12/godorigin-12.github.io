Welcome to My Notes.
The data we store are user's email and notes created by user as part of app functionality.
You can request to delete either:
1. All your notes and keep email so that you still have a account in our app
2. Your account in which your email and all your notes will be removed from our server

To request any of above you can send email to us at godorigin12@gmail.com with the type of request (1 or 2 from above) along with the email id registered in the app.

We will then manually remove your data from our server and send you acknowledgement.
